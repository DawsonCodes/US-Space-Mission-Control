Mission status legend

The launch statuses surfaced by Launch Library 2, in plain language.

  • Go for LaunchCleared and on track for the scheduled time.
  • To Be ConfirmedDate is likely but awaiting final confirmation.
  • To Be DeterminedDate is tentative and may still move.
  • HoldCountdown paused; launch is temporarily held.
  • SuccessThe launch completed successfully.
  • FailureThe launch failed to reach its objective.
  • Partial FailurePartially successful — some objectives were missed.
  • Unknown statusNo recognized status was provided.

Statuses can change as a launch approaches.
